Offered Courses

Undergraduate Courses

Introduction to Radiological Engineering

Focuses on fundamental engineering concepts and primary physics principles of radiation, exploring diverse industry application fields.

  • Target: Undergraduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits

Radiation Health Physics

Studies biological impacts of ionizing radiation exposures on human bodies along with radiation protection and safety control rules.

  • Target: Undergraduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits

Graduate Courses

Radiation Engineering

Deals with in-depth engineering knowledge regarding radiation generation mechanisms, matter interactions, metrics, and systems.

  • Target: Graduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits

Radiation Beam Application Engineering

Covers engineering skills for generation, direction adjustments, and applications of high-energy radiation beams in industries and clinics.

  • Target: Graduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits

Radiation Image Processing

An intense study on diverse digital image processing and analytics frameworks aimed at acquiring, enhancing, and resolving medical/industrial radiographic data.

  • Target: Graduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits

Radiation Physics and Shielding

Acquires advanced theories regarding radiation transport physics alongside professional layouts for structural radiation shielding architectures.

  • Target: Graduate
  • Credits: 3 Credits
