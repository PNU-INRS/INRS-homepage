About Our Lab

Intelligent Neutron and Radiological Science Lab (INRS Lab)

Pusan National University, Department of Nuclear Engineering

The Intelligent Neutron and Radiological Science Lab (INRS Lab) aims to develop advanced imaging technologies utilizing radiation system design and neutron engineering. We pursue both academic breakthroughs and practical applications through radiation-based system optimization, neutron imaging techniques, and machine learning fusion.

Core Research Fields

  • Radiation System Design & Optimization
    • Development and optimization of X-ray and neutron-based radiation systems.
    • Design of medical imaging and industrial diagnostic systems using radiation tech.
    • Analysis of diverse radiation interactions to implement high-end systems.
  • Neutron Imaging & Application Technologies
    • Phase-contrast and dark-field imaging using neutron interferometry.
    • Neutron-based non-destructive testing (NDT) and stress analysis applications.
    • Commercialization of neutron imaging tech for industrial and medical use.
  • Machine Learning & Radiation Imaging Convergence
    • Quality enhancement and reconstruction of radiation images via machine learning.
    • Deep learning-based image denoising and data analysis automation.
    • Optimization of radiation and neutron data through artificial intelligence.
  • Multidisciplinary Radiation Research
    • Development of multi-modal imaging and diagnostics using radiation and neutrons.
    • Exploring novel imaging methodologies via technical fusion.

Lab Vision

"INRS Lab: Illuminating the Unseen World with Radiation and Neutrons."

We aim to achieve both scientific innovation and real-world impact through state-of-the-art research.

  • Technical Convergence: Proposing a new paradigm by combining radiation technology with neutron engineering.
  • Practical Application: Developing practical engineering technologies derived from academic insights.
  • Future Talents: Cultivating convergent researchers who will lead future fields.

Key Achievements

  • International Publications: Numerous papers published in world-renowned journals such as Scientific Reports, Physical Review Letters, and Journal of Instrumentation.
  • Technology Development: X-ray/neutron system designs, and AI-based image optimization techniques.
  • Global Collaborations: Active industrial and academic collaborations to deploy technologies in real fields.

Our Strengths

  • State-of-the-art Equipment & Environment
    • Advanced imaging instruments specialized for radiation system layouts.
    • Abundant research infrastructure provided by Pusan National University.
  • Global Research Network
    • Joint projects with global institutes (e.g., National Institute of Standards and Technology, USA) and industries.
    • Established networks in global conferences.
  • Diverse Career Opportunities
    • Alumni successfully entering industries, public research institutes, and medical centers.
    • Opportunities to join highly innovative, practical projects.
